# 🏢 Sierra Estates Real Estate Consultancy
> **The Future of Real Estate: AI Discovers.. We Advise!**

Sierra Estates is not just a traditional real estate brokerage; it is a **PropTech consultancy** that merges the superpowers of Artificial Intelligence in data analysis with the luxurious human touch in negotiation and trust-building.

---

## 🔄 The AI-Human Synergy Workflow
Our system is designed to operate like a precision machine, where each phase seamlessly hands over the task to the next:

1. **Automated Scraping (The Hunter):** Continuous scanning of WhatsApp groups to automatically extract owner listings and populate them into Google Sheets.
2. **Human Validation:** Inside Sales Specialists filter the data, contact the owners for verification, and publish authenticated ads.
3. **Intelligent Triage (Liela Bot):** Our virtual assistant "Liela" instantly receives inbound ad inquiries, qualifies client requirements, and suggests top 3 matching units.
4. **Field Closing:** The human closer receives a fully qualified "Hot Lead", meets them on the ground, and successfully closes the deal.

## 👥 The Human Elite: Roles & Financial Structure

### 1. Inside Sales & Listing Specialist
*The backbone of the company, responsible for acquiring units and qualifying clients over the phone.*
- **Target:** Onboard 50 owners monthly (20 must have 'Available' units).
- **KPI-based Salary Split:**
  - **30%** for hitting the acquisition and inventory target.
  - **30%** for closing targets (2 rentals or 1 sale).
  - **30%** for discipline and daily CRM updates.
  - **10%** for positive attitude and teamwork.
- **Commissions:** 10% from the tenant side, 15% from the owner side (plus 10% & 12.5% internal split deals to encourage teamwork).

### 2. Field Closer
*The field warrior who closes deals face-to-face.*
- **System:** Commission-based Only (No basic salary).
- **Escalating Commissions (Based on Daily Effort):**
  - **15%** for conducting and closing 1 viewing in a day.
  - **20%** for conducting 2 viewings on the same day.
  - **25%** for conducting 3 or more viewings on the same day (to incentivize speed).

### 3. Team Leader
*The spearhead who intervenes in major deals and guides the team.*
- **Target Basic Salary:** 10,000 EGP monthly.
- **Target:** Close (2 sales) or (4 rentals) monthly.
- **Proportional Salary System:**
  - The salary is paid proportionally to the target achieved (e.g., hitting 50% of the target = 5,000 EGP paid + personal commissions).
