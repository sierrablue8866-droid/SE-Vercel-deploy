# Job Description & Requirements: Inside Sales & Listing Specialist (Owner Relations)

## Objective
You are the core engine of Sierra Estatese. The company relies on you to secure direct-from-owner real estate inventory, register it, transform it into attractive marketing campaigns, and conduct initial client triage before handing hot leads to the Field Closer.

## Daily Tasks (Workflow)
1. **Data Reception:** Monitor the Google Sheet populated by the AI Scraper Bot with new owner numbers extracted from WhatsApp.
2. **Validation & Qualification:** Call the owner to verify intent, obtain full unit details (photos, final price, conditions), and convince them to list exclusively or directly with the company.
3. **Listing:** Accurately enter the unit's data into the Antigravity CRM to make it ready for display.
4. **Marketing:** Write compelling ad copy and publish it on marketing platforms (Property Finder, Social Media).
5. **Initial Triage:** Respond to clients coming from the ads, qualify them over the phone, and schedule a confirmed viewing appointment to hand over to the Closer.

## KPIs & Salary Structure
The salary is performance-based to incentivize productivity, divided as follows:
- **30% (Growth Target):** Add 50 new owners monthly, with at least 20 owners having currently 'Available' units.
- **30% (Closing Target):** Achieve a closing target (scheduling viewings that result in 2 rentals or 1 sale per month).
- **30% (Discipline):** Strict adherence to working hours and error-free CRM data entry.
- **10% (Attitude & Teamwork):** Assisting colleagues and facilitating the field team's tasks.

## Commissions
*(Calculated from the total company commission upon successful deal closure)*
- **10%** from the tenant's side (for successfully receiving the client and setting the appointment).
- **15%** from the owner's side (if the unit belongs to your specific owner portfolio).
- **5%** if the unit is brought through another broker.
- **10% (Split Deal):** If you listed the unit in the system, but another colleague brought the client.
- **12.5% (Split Deal):** If you brought the client, but the unit belongs to another colleague's portfolio.

## Job Requirements
1. **Experience:** Prior experience in Telesales, Customer Service, or Real Estate Brokerage is preferred.
2. **Soft Skills:**
   - Ambitious personality with a relentless drive to hit targets (Hunter Mindset).
   - High persuasion and negotiation skills over the phone.
   - Excellent communication skills and professionalism when dealing with VIP clients.
3. **Technical Skills:** Fast learner with the ability to use CRM systems and MS Office/Google Workspace.
4. **Discipline:** Highly organized with exceptional attention to detail in data entry.

## Working Hours
- **Daily:** 11:00 AM to 7:00 PM.
- **Saturday:** Half-day (Work From Home).
- **Days Off:** 1 full day off per week + 1 additional half-day off mid-week.
